Our Story

BORN FROM
THE VALLEY

Edinburg, Texas sits in the heart of the Rio Grande Valley — where the sun blazes, the community thrives, and the need for clean, refreshing hydration is felt every single day. Valley Water Co. was built right here, for the people right here.

We noticed something simple: the shelves were full of brands from elsewhere — brands that had never seen a Valley summer, never felt that 105°F afternoon heat, never understood what it means to truly need a cold, clean drink. So we built our own.

Valley Water Co. is more than a beverage company. It's a local brand that belongs to this community — a brand you'll recognize from your neighborhood, your campus, and your corner store.

Mission Statement

OUR MISSION

"To hydrate and energize the Rio Grande Valley community by delivering affordable, high-quality, and locally rooted water-based beverages that reflect the culture, spirit, and pride of South Texas."

Vision

WHERE WE'RE
HEADED

We see a future where Valley Water Co. is the first name that comes to mind when anyone in the Rio Grande Valley reaches for a drink. A truly local brand — on every shelf, in every home, at every game.

Short Term

Launch our full product line across Edinburg and establish vending machine presence in UTRGV, parks, gyms, and shopping centers to build immediate brand recognition.

Mid Term

Secure shelf space in H-E-B, Walmart, and local convenience stores. Capture 15% of Hidalgo County's bottled water market.

Long Term

Expand distribution across the entire Rio Grande Valley and become the premier locally-owned beverage brand in South Texas.
